Lab 4: The MOST Impactful Fight

Iron Man vs. Thanos

Using his advanced nano-tech armor, Iron Man took the fight directly to Thanos on Titan. His suit's versatility allowed for several attacks with blasters, shields, and blades.

In a climactic moment, Tony Stark managed to do what no one else had: he made Thanos bleed. This critical moment proved that this was the most impactful fight against Thanos.
